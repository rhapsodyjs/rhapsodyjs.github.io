A NodeJS HMVC framework for epic apps!

Build high-performance, safe, organized and epic apps!

RhapsodyJS allows you to create scalable apps with custom REST APIs, real-time with WebSockets, and has built-in REST APIs for your models, you can even enable cluster module for improve the scalability of your app.

It's highly plugable, you can choose what database you want to use, use community middlewares for your controllers and REST APIs or create your owns, choose your favorite template engine, allows i18n, and generates automatically models for your prefered front-end framework!
